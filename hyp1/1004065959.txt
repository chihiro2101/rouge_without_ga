the world the alienation has two effects : the belief that the disorder of the world can be transcended by extraordinary insight , learning , or knowledge , called a gnostic speculation by voegelin ( the gnostics themselves referred to it as gnosis ) the desire to create and implement a policy to actualize the speculation , or as voegelin described it , to immanentize the eschaton , to create a
