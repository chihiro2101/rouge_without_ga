in the wrasses ( the family labridae ) , sex change is from female to male , with the largest female of the harem changing into a male and taking over the harem upon the disappearance of the previous dominant male several medical conditions can result in an apparent sex change in humans , where the appearance at birth is somewhat , mostly , or completely of one sex , but changes over the course of a lifetime to being somewhat , mostly , or completely of the other sex as a result , the person has some or all of the physical characteristics of a female , despite having the genetic makeup of a male the term '' sex change '' to refer to sex reassignment surgery , that is , a set of medical procedures undergone by transgender people to alter their sexual characteristics from male to female or from female to male , is considered offensive and inaccurate by many in the transgender community the term is also sometimes used for the medical procedures intersex people undergo , or , more often , are subjected to as children the term '' sex change '' is sometimes also used for the whole process of changing gender role ( '' living as a woman '' instead of living as a man , or vice versa ) , not limited to medical procedures ( this process is often much more important to transgender people than the medical procedures themselves , although medically induced changes and surgeries may be needed to make a change of gender role possible , both socially and legally ; they can also have a very significant impact on the person 's well-being sex in humans is usually determined by four factors : chromosomes gonads ( ovary and/or testicles and the associated gametes , eggs and sperm ) hormone status sex organ and secondary sex characteristics not all of these factors can be changed : chromosomes can not be changed gonads can be removed , but not replaced hormone status can be changed existing secondary sex characteristics can to some extent be changed ;
