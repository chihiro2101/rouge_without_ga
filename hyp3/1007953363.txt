intendant of justice , police and finance the instructions dealt with mutual relations of church and state , discussed the question of assistance to the recently created west india company , and justice ; the settlement of the colony and the advisability of concentrating the population ; the importance of fostering trade and industry ; the question of tithes for the maintenance of the church
