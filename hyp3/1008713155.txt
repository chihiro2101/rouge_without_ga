agrarian , but by the middle of the nineteenth century , burcht had become the site of heavy industry among the industries established there at the time were a guano factory , a linoleum factory , a 1914 and burcht and zwijndrecht , like all the german-occupied communities in belgium , suffered greatly for the next four years from food shortages by the end of the war , 80 young people from
