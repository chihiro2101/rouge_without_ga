butt replaced the association with a new body , the home rule league , which he regarded as a pressure-group , rather than a political party in the meantime charles stewart parnell had joined the league , with more radical ideas than most of the incumbent home rulers , and was elected to parliament in a by-election in county meath in 1875 butt had failed to win substantial concessions at
